        Leaderboard Calculations & Rules
        • Prayer Score: Sum of points based on prayer status (Jamaat = 2.0, Individual = 1.0, Qaza = 0.5, Missed = 0.0).
        • Prayer Completion %: Count of completed prayers (Jamaat, Individual, or Qaza) ÷ Total expected prayers (5 per logged day) × 100.
        • Submission Rate %: Number of days with submitted tracker logs ÷ Total expected days in the period (7 days for Weekly, 30 days for Monthly) × 100.
        • Ranking Order: Students are ranked 1st by highest Prayer Score, 2nd by highest Submission Rate %, and 3rd by the most recent active log date.
